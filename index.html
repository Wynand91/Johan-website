ABOUT US

Johan’s Electrical is based in Strand, providing services throughout Helderberg and its surrounding areas. Our Primary goal is to satisfy each and every one of our client’s needs, no matter how big or small it may be. Quality workmanship, dedication, professionalism and attention to detail is something that we strive for in every job. We are a fully authorized company and registered with all relevant authorities. Our qualified team’s knowledge and experience of everything electrical allow us to give our best in every aspect of the job – be it domestic, commercial or industrial.

Vision

To continue growing our company by maintaining an excellent reputation of reliability, whilst meeting and exceeding our client’s expectations and as a result become a industry leader for providing electrical services.

Mission

Our mission is to provide our clients with services like no other company can. We take pride in our approach to any project, planning every little detail to accommodate for every customer need, all this whilst minimizing costs.
